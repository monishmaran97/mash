How We Work

Our Design Process

A

Analyze

Site, climate, and client needs are assessed through data-driven analysis before a single line is drawn.

G

Generate

Computational tools are used for form-finding within site and regulatory constraints to explore optimal solutions.

R

Refine

Material efficiency and buildability are stress-tested and optimised for practical, real-world outcomes.

D

Deliver

Detailed drawings and specifications are produced, ready for construction with full execution support.
